About One Piece

Ruthless pirates rule the oceans and seas, but only the most agile and successful have the opportunity to go in search of a mysterious treasure known as the "One Piece", which once belonged to the legendary sea robber - Gold Roger. Several years after the death of Gold Roger, a young and determined Monkey D. Luffy strives to be the first to find the "One Piece" in order to subsequently receive the unofficial title of the Pirate King. As young Monkey gains confidence in his own abilities, he surrounds himself with a motley crew of pirates, including the cabin boy Koby and the thief Nami, and sets off to meet amazing adventures, believing that luck will smile on him.
